Indian Sign Language

ISL is a natural language used by the Deaf community in India. It has its own grammar, structure, and cultural importance.

Learning ISL promotes inclusion and meaningful communication.

ISL is not signed English.
Facial expressions matter in ISL.
Consistency builds understanding.
